One-pan Honey Garlic Chicken

4.9 from 25 reviews
  • Prep time: 15 min
  • Cook time: 37 min
  • Yield: 5
Print Recipe Pin Recipe
Ingredients
For the whole
  • 6 bone-in, skin-on chicken thighs
  • salt, to taste
  • pepper, to taste
  • 1 tablespoon unsalted butter
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • 1 tablespoon brown sugar
  • ¼ cup honey (85 g)
  • teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 lb green beans (450 g)

Description

One Pan Honey Garlic Chicken and Vegetables – Chicken breasts, potatoes and green beans all cook together in under 30 minutes and in one pan. As simple and easy as a healthy dinner recipe gets!

Instructions

  • 01. Preheat oven to 400˚F (200˚C)
  • 02. Season chicken thighs with salt and pepper.
  • 03. Melt 1 tablespoon butter in a large ovenproof skillet over medium heat. Add chicken, skin-side down, and sear both sides until golden brown. (The skin side will take much longer, in order to properly render the fat and crisp up the skin.)
  • 04. Remove chicken thighs and set aside. Pour out any excess fat, but leave some in for the sauce.
  • 05. Add garlic, stir until fragrant, then add brown sugar, honey, thyme, and oregano, and stir. Reduce heat to low.
  • 06. Return chicken to the skillet. Coat the chicken in the sauce.
  • 07. Add green beans to skillet.
  • 08. Bake for 25 minutes or until chicken is cooked through.
